9413 sophia ave

choreographed deconstruction and performed maintenance of the built environment

9413 Sophia Ave is an address and a durational performance. The project operates as a case study which choreographically deconstructs and biocycles a home which was condemned by the Cuyahoga Land Bank, exhibiting maintenance of the built environment as art and resulting materially in a sculptural artifact. Physically peeling back layers of a structure and revealing a network of stories tying back to the site, the performance engages with the life cycle and ephemerality of built structures.

9413 Sophia Ave aims, through its specificity, to tell a broader story about the built environment.
